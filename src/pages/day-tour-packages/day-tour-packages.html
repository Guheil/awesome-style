Frequently Asked Questions About Our La Union Beach Resort Day Tour

What is a day tour?

A day tour usually occurs on the same day or within a few days. You go on the tour, and then you can go home on the same day.

How can I spend a day in La Union?

You can spend a day in La Union learning how to surf and trying different kinds of food, especially those at Awesome Hotel and our Lobby Cafe. You can also explore other La Union gems, like the other restaurants by the shoreline or the museums, historical landmarks, and tourism sites nearby.

Why should we visit La Union?

Visit La Union for the food, the waves, the rich history, the culture, and the experience! You should also visit La Union for Awesome Hotel!

What is the best month to go to La Union?

It depends on what you are coming to La Union for. March to May is excellent for summer there. For surfing, go from October to April due to the waves.

Which beach is less crowded in La Union?

While La Union’s popularity means most beaches see visitors. However, the private shores near our San Juan La Union beach resort tend to be less crowded, offering a more tranquil experience.

Does La Union have a white sand beach?

La Union is renowned for its stunning coastline, but the beaches here aren’t typically known for powdery white sand. Instead, they boast a unique and beautiful golden hue, creating a picturesque and natural backdrop for your resort day tour in La Union. This golden sand, combined with the clear waters, offers a distinctive coastal charm that is truly captivating and perfect for photographs.

Which La Union beach is better at night?

When the sun sets, San Juan comes alive, offering a vibrant nightlife scene with a variety of bars, restaurants, and entertainment options. It’s the ideal choice for those looking for excitement and social interaction. For guests seeking a more relaxed evening, our resort’s private beach provides a tranquil ambiance, allowing you to unwind and enjoy the peaceful sounds of the ocean.

Which is better, San Juan or San Fernando La Union?

Both San Juan and San Fernando offer distinct experiences. San Juan is famous for its world-class surfing waves and lively, energetic atmosphere, attracting surfers and beach lovers alike. San Fernando, as the provincial capital, offers a more laid-back vibe and a glimpse into the local culture and heritage. Our conveniently located day tour beach resort in La Union allows you to easily explore and appreciate the unique charm of both destinations.
