MENU HIGHLIGHTS

At our hotel in La Union, the breakfast buffet is a feast for the senses, featuring a diverse selection of international and local favorites, all prepared with the freshest ingredients. From hearty classics to lighter fare, there’s something to tantalize every taste bud.

WESTERN DELIGHTS

Fuel your morning with familiar comforts like fluffy scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, savory sausages, and golden hash browns. We also offer a selection of freshly baked pastries, including croissants, muffins, and danishes, perfect with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or tea.

Filipino Favorites

Experience the flavors of the Philippines with traditional breakfast dishes such as tapsilog (cured beef, fried rice, and fried egg), longganisa (sweet sausage), tocino (sweet cured pork), and pancit (noodles). Don’t forget to try our flavorful adobo (braised meat in soy sauce and vinegar), a Filipino culinary staple.

Healthy Choices

For those seeking a lighter start, our buffet also features a variety of healthy options. Enjoy fresh fruits, yogurt, granola, and a selection of cereals. We also offer made-to-order omelets with your choice of fillings, ensuring a nutritious and personalized breakfast.

Beverage Selection

Quench your thirst with our wide array of beverages, including freshly squeezed juices, brewed coffee, tea, and hot chocolate. We also offer a selection of soft drinks and bottled water.

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Restaurant in San Juan, La Union

Is your restaurant family-friendly?

Absolutely! We welcome families with open arms. We have high chairs for our younger guests, and our menu includes kid-friendly options to satisfy even the pickiest eaters. While we don’t have a dedicated play area, the beachfront location provides a safe and spacious environment for children to explore.

What is the latest time guests can come in for breakfast?

Guests may enter the breakfast buffet until 10:00 AM, which is the last seating time. After that, the buffet begins closing and no new guests will be accommodated.

Are large groups or families welcome?

Absolutely! Our dining area can accommodate larger parties, and our team can assist in arranging seating for groups.

Is there parking available for walk-in breakfast guests?

Yes, guests arriving by car can enjoy ample on-site parking for convenience.

Is reservation required or recommended?

Reservations are not required for breakfast, but guests may contact us for special arrangements or group bookings.

Do you offer vegetarian, vegan, or allergy-friendly options?

A selection of dishes is prepared daily to accommodate different dietary needs. Guests with restrictions may inform our staff for assistance.

Can I request a seat with a specific view (poolside / indoor)?

Yes, you may request a preferred seating area such as poolside or indoor. While we can’t guarantee specific seats during peak hours, our team will do their best to accommodate your request based on availability at the time of your visit.
