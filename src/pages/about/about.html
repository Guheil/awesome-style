Our Hotel

Escape to your own slice of paradise at our 4-star beachfront luxury hotel in San Juan, La Union. Located on the serene side of town, Awesome Hotel offers stunning ocean views, 113 spacious and well-appointed rooms, and world-class amenities, our hotel is the perfect place to relax and recharge.

Green Initiatives

Recognized by Love La Union in 2023 as a Green Tourism Enterprise, Awesome Hotel is committed to preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings and protecting the environment.

It is our goal to contribute positive impact in the tourism and hospitality industry with our green initiatives in our operations such as use of solar power, reuse of water for non-potable purposes like irrigation, reduction of single-use plastics, installation of water refill stations for guests, and electric vehicle charging stations.

We are proud to be doing our part to protect the environment, and we invite our guests to join us in our commitment to sustainability.
