Yes, absolutely! While we have a special Game Day menu, we offer a variety of delicious bar snacks and light bites throughout the day.  Feel free to ask your server or browse our full menu above.

Yes, you can! Our bar is an excellent venue for private parties and events. We offer customizable packages to suit your needs, whether it’s a birthday celebration, a team building activity, or a cocktail reception. Yes, we do! For groups looking for a more exclusive experience, we offer bottle service with a selection of premium spirits and champagnes. Our team will be happy to assist you in choosing the perfect bottle to celebrate your special occasion.

Can we request custom cocktails?

While our menu features a wide range of handcrafted cocktails, our skilled bartenders are also happy to create custom cocktails based on your preferences. Just let us know your favorite flavors and spirits, and we’ll whip up something special just for you!
